[[BOAT notes]] are a "Block Of Atomic Thought" which are solitary objects not connected too well. They are a precursor to [[Evergreen notes]] with the following properties:

- **few Links** (if at all)
- **less defined** (rough)
- **newly created**
- But, with a **promise** of **potential**
